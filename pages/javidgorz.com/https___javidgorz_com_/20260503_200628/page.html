Why JavidGorz

Zero Logging

We collect nothing. No IP addresses, no traffic data, no connection timestamps. We have nothing to hand over because we store nothing.

Bespoke Protocol Engine

Purpose-built routing technology that intelligently adapts to network conditions. Designed specifically to navigate the most restrictive environments.

Smart Auto-Connect

Automatically finds the fastest available route. If one path is blocked, it instantly tries the next. No manual configuration needed.

Works with Local Apps

Your everyday Iranian apps and local services continue to work at full speed. Only restricted traffic is handled by JavidGorz.

Free & Community Supported

JavidGorz is currently free to use. We are looking for volunteers and supporters to help sustain this project. Get in touch if you want to help.

Kill Switch

If the connection drops, the kill switch blocks all traffic instantly. Protects against network changes and sudden disconnections.
